RuneScape - Smooth Movement

James Hardy Jul 24 2021-07-24T13:00:00+01:00
Jul 24 2021-07-24T22:59:16+01:00 1 min

Back in late 2019 I worked on an improvement to the motion of player characters and NPCs in RuneScape. Players and NPCs in RuneScape are aligned to a grid, and have historically moved in straight lines and at fixed speeds, so there were some literal rough-edges to be smoothed out. I already wrote a blog post about this on the official RuneScape website, so I’ll just leave a link to it here!
